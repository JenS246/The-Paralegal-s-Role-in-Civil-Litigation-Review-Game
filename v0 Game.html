Paralegal Pathways: Civil Litigation Challenge

Current Stage: Pre-Filing Investigation

Welcome to Paralegal Pathways

You've just been hired as a paralegal at Blackstone & Associates, a civil litigation firm in Philadelphia. Your supervising attorney, Sarah Chen, will guide you through your first case.

In this simulation, you'll navigate through all stages of a civil litigation case, making decisions about your responsibilities as a paralegal. Your choices will affect the outcome of the case and your performance evaluation.

Game Structure:

  • Progress through 8 stages of civil litigation
  • Make decisions about paralegal tasks at each stage
  • Earn points for correct professional decisions
  • Learn about proper procedures and best practices
  • Receive feedback on your choices

Your Case:

Johnson v. Philadelphia General Hospital - A potential medical malpractice case where the client, Robert Johnson, believes he received negligent care that resulted in complications after surgery.

Litigation Stages:

Pre-Filing Investigation

Gather and organize information before filing a lawsuit

Pleadings Phase

Prepare and file initial court documents

Discovery Process

Exchange information between parties through formal procedures

Pre-Trial Motions

Prepare and respond to motions before trial

Settlement Negotiations

Attempt to resolve the case without trial

Trial Preparation

Organize evidence and prepare witnesses for trial

Trial Proceedings

Support attorneys during courtroom proceedings

Post-Trial Procedures

Handle matters after the verdict
